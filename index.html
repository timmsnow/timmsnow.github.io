About

I'm a Full-stack web developer with a penchant for underwater exploration who's motivated by the dynamic capabilities of coding. I believe that we can change the world with the power of CODE. There is an infinite need for code and infinite ways it can be applied. I am extremely motivated by the enumerable possibilities, and by the projects and initiatives that can be undertaken with the right tools and the right mindest.

Tech is an industry, but it can should MUST also be a gateway. There is no reason why we cannot marry the pursuit of innovation with the values of humanity. I want to strive to contribute to the greater good of our planet. I want tech to lift up and empower communities across the globe. I want tech to work for all of us, for a more equitable future... and I'm ready to work with anyone that's trying to do the same.
